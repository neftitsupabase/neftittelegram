To ensure sustainable token distribution, fair participation, and long-term user engagement, NEFTIT implements a tiered staking system with fixed daily NEFT reward rates and maximum stakeable NFT limits per tier. This model discourages farming by whales, preserves the value of higher-tier NFTs, and motivates users to strategically burn and upgrade their NFTs.

#### Key Design Benefits

**1. Anti-Farming Mechanism**\
By capping the number of NFTs that can be staked per tier, NEFTIT prevents wallet stacking and points dumping. This ensures fair distribution among both early adopters and new users.

**2. Upgrade Incentivization**\
Although lower-tier NFTs like Common and Rare can be staked in larger numbers, they still offer lower monthly returns compared to higher-tier NFTs. The structure encourages users to burn and upgrade NFTs to access higher-value tiers.

**3. Strategic Progression**\
Users must plan their strategy: hold many lower-tier NFTs or burn them to climb into more exclusive, higher-yield staking tiers like Silver or Gold. This adds a layer of game theory and long-term engagement.

**4. Controlled Token Emission**\
With hard staking caps, NEFTIT can maintain predictable and controlled token output, protecting the overall economy from hyperinflation.



#### Final Notes

* **Gold NFTs are limited to 1 per wallet** to maintain their elite status. Their rewards are balanced to be the highest while still justifying the significant burn effort required.
* **Silver and Platinum NFTs** remain highly rewarding, but are more accessible for mid-level users.
* Users are encouraged to participate in campaigns to earn more NFTs and maximize their staking potential within the limit constraints.

This staking framework ensures NEFTIT remains a fair, engaging, and scalable reward ecosystem as it grows across chains and user communities.
