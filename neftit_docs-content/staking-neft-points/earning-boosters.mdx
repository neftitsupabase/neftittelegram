# Earning Boosters

1. **Streak Bonus**
   * Stake NFTs continuously for 7+ days? Get bonus NEFT Points per day as loyalty reward.
2. **Multi-NFT Combo**
   * Stake multiple NFTs of different rarities = combo boost.
   * Example: Staking Common + Rare + Legendary gives some **% of NEFT Point multiplier**.
3. **Referral Multiplier**
   * Bring in friends? users will get NEFT points for inviting friends.
4. **Event-Based Boosts**
   * Special campaigns may get reward of NEFT points
5. Achievements
   * complete achievements to get more NEFT points. ex. hold NFTs, burn NFTs, Stake NFTs, Buy/sell NFTs, updating profile, etc.
