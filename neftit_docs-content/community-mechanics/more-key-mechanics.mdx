# More Key Mechanics

1. **Invite-to-Earn Mechanism**
   * Users who invite active members can earn bonus NEFT points.
   * Prevents spam by tracking invited user activity.
2. **Bot-Proof Systems**
   * Only genuine activity (verified) is rewarded.
   * No “spam to win” systems allowed.
3. **Dynamic Campaigns**
   * Projects can customize campaigns for deeper engagement (like quizzes, polls, video posts).
4. **Campaign Voting (Coming Soon)**
   * OG and some discord role holders can vote on which projects to list.
   * Gives more power to believers and filters quality projects.
5. **Rarity-Based Visibility**
   * Users who earn higher rarities get highlighted on the leaderboard and Discord.
6. **Daily/Weekly Leaderboards**
   * Adds competition.
   * Top users may win exclusive NFTs, codes, or roles.
