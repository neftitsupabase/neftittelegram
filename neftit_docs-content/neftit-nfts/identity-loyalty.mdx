# Identity & Loyalty

NEFTIT NFTs are designed to act like a **Web3 resume**, they show what you’ve supported, how active you’ve been, and how far you’ve progressed in the ecosystem. They carry:

* **Visual identity of projects supported**
* **User contribution proof**
* **Staking history**
* **Burn history**
* **Badge achievements**

Over time, your NEFTIT NFT collection becomes your **Web3 merit badge chest**.
