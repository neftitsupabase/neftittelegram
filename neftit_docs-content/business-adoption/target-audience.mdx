# Target Audience / User Personas

NEFTIT is designed to support multiple user groups across the Web3 ecosystem:

1. **Early-Stage Potential Web3 Projects:**\
   Potential Projects lacking a strong community, marketing budget, or user base can use NEFTIT to grow organically through task-based campaigns and NFT incentives.
2. **NFT Collectors & Gamified Users:**\
   Individuals who enjoy collecting, upgrading, and earning NFTs through engaging activities like social tasks, quizzes, or on-chain interactions.
3. **Community Managers & Marketers:**\
   Teams looking for a simple and effective way to run NFT-based community campaigns without dealing with smart contract complexity.
4. **DeFi, GameFi, and DAO Projects:**\
   Projects that want to bootstrap community loyalty by rewarding users with NFTs for off-chain or on-chain participation.
