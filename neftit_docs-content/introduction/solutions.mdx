
# Solutions


## The Solution: How NEFTIT Solves It All

**Free NFT Based Campaigns to Onboard Real Users**
- Projects launch social tasks on NEFTIT.
- Users complete tasks and earn NFTs for free, no cost to users.
- Filters bots using internal system and reward logic (badges, rarity boosts).

**NEFTIT = Launchpad + Growth Engine + Reward System**
- All-in-one platform to boost visibility, run viral campaigns, and reward loyal users.
- Projects can list without complex setup.
- NEFTIT handles engagement, growth tracking, and NFT distribution.

**Discovery Layer for Hidden Gems**
- Users explore verified upcoming projects across chains.
- Community feedback + OG voting highlights quality campaigns.
- No noise, just real projects with potential.

**NFTs with Daily Utility: Stake, Burn, Upgrade**
- Collected NFTs can be staked for NEFT points.
- Users can burn & upgrade NFTs to reach “NEFTIT Gold NFT”.
- NFTs evolve with the users, making them feel earned, not airdropped.

**Gamified Loyalty System**
- Tasks, codes, badges all add to your NEFT points.
- The more you engage, the more valuable your journey becomes.
- Campaigns become quests, not chores.

**Special Role-Based Rewards (OGs, Early Joiners, etc.)**
- Community roles earn access to exclusive campaigns, rare NFTs, some governance power.
- Loyalty is recognized. Contributions matter.

**Multichain NFT Distribution System**
- Projects choose their preferred chain.
- NEFTIT automatically mints/distributes NFTs on that chain after campaign ends.
- Users get true multichain collectibles.

**Web2-Friendly Experience**
- Easy to understand.
- Social logins, simple UI, clear onboarding.
- Education & hand-holding to turn casual users into Web3 natives.
